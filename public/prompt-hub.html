♥   New here? How to use this hub ▼
  1. Pick a library. Use the three tabs just below to switch between The Dollhouse Hub (prompts for running the agency), the AI Cashflow Masterclass (plays for finding new clients), and the AI Money Vault (lead lists, outreach, AI-search audits, agents and money plays from the masterclass).
  2. Fill in Client Details once. Open the Client Details box and type in the business name, niche, city, and so on. Every prompt fills that info in for you — green means it's filled in, yellow means you still need to add it before sending.
  3. Find the prompt you need. Tap Categories to open the full list and jump to a section, or type in the search box (e.g. "email," "carousel," "leads").
  4. Follow the Step numbers. Work top to bottom. When prompts share the same Step number, they're options — just pick one and move on.
  5. Copy, paste, done. Hit Copy Prompt, paste it into GHL Ask AI, then tap Mark done so you can see your progress at the top.

That's it. You don't need to memorize anything — work through the steps in order and the hub keeps you on track. The bar at the top shows how far you've gotten.
